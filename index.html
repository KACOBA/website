Kagoma Community Based Organisation (KACOBA) is a registered not for profit, Community Based and Non Government Oganisation, (Certficate number S.5914/3111-NGO Board Uganda), founded in 1995 and first registered in 2000. The organisation's major aim is to improve the quality of life of the neediest and marginalised groups of people in Uganda.

Some of KACOBA's orphaned children participate in tree planting as a means of mitigation.


We believe that with a good enviroment, good health, good nutrition, Education, among others, some one can enjoy precious life. Hence; We believe that the implementation of the eight (8) Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) is the responsbility of every one.

KACOBA started in only one District of Jinja, but currently it is operating in twelve (12) districts of Jinja, Mayuge, Kamuli, Kampala, Wakiso, Nakaseke, Nakasongola, Mityana, Gulu, Mpigi, Luwero and Mubende. The projects include: I am a person who matters, HEN project, Women's Initiatives, Youth Initiatives, Community an Home based Health Care, Alcoholic Anonymous, among others.
